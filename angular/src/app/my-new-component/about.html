My First Heading

About An online photo management and sharing application! To enable you to share memories (photos) with your friends, family and whomever you wish. (Free subscription enables you to share with subscribers only.) To enable ways of organising your photos so you can have everlasting memories. An option available to put a date of the photo... when the memory occurred. Organising your photos into albums with ‘Adding’ , ‘Deleting’, and ‘Sharing’ options.
